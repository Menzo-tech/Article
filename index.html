Chicken-peanut stew Article

Mmenim Inyang

Creamy peanut stew are a familiar dish in West Africa cooking and my chicken version is a delicious one to try as a new family dinner. Somewhere between a hearty casserole and curry -pinch And Swirl, this mouth watering bowl is packed with tender chicken, sweet potato, and pepper, all cooked with peanut sauce

My Meal

2 tbsp olive olive, 1 sliced onions, 2 medium sweet potato, 1 chopped red pepper, 1 chicken stock cube, 500g skinless and boneless chicken thigh fillet (cut into bite-size pieces), 2 garlic cloves(crushed) 5cm piece ginger (peeled and grated), half tbsp ground coriander, half tbsp ground cumin, 1 tbsp crushed chillies, 30g fresh coriander leave few peanut to prinkle (optional)
